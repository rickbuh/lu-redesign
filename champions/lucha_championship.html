LU Championship

Prince Puma

Won January 7, 2015

Episode 1.09 | Aztec Warfare

In the first-ever Aztec Warfare match, Boyle Heights' own Prince Puma outlasted nineteen other luchadors, including Fenix, Drago, Mil Muertes, Sexy Star, and Pentagon Jr. Finally, Puma pinned Johnny Mundo with a 630 Senton to become the inaugural Lucha Underground Champion.

Mil Muertes

Won August 5, 2015

Episode 1.39 | Ultima Lucha Part 2

In the main event of Ultima Lucha I, Prince Puma fought the behemoth from beyond the grave, Mil Muertes. With the help of Catrina and her magical stone, Mil summoned the power to spear Puma out of the ring and through a table. The action was so intense that neither Puma's 630 or Mil's Flatliner were able to end the match (nobody had ever kicked out of either move before). In the end, the man of a thousand deaths finished Puma with a Super Flatliner off the top rope.

Fenix

Won March 16, 2016

Episode 2.08 | Life After Death

After recapturing the Gift of the Gods from King Cuemo, Fenix was quick to redeem his championship opportunity. Having been embroiled in a bitter rivalry with Mil Muertes throughout Lucha Underground's first season, it came as no surprise that their match tumed into an all-out brawl. After a dive from the crowd, a powerbomb onto the announce table, and Mil Muertes' bloody near-unmasking, Fenix finally scored the pinfall with a bridge.

Matanza Cueto

Won March 23, 2016

Episode 2.09 | Aztec Warfare II

Fenix's reign as Lucha Underground Champion was short-lived. The following week, he was forced to defend his title by entering #1 in Aztec Warfare II, where the debuting monster, Matanza Cueto laid waste to Fenix with a devastating Wrath of the Gods. But Fenix was only the first of Matanza's victims. Matanza plowed through eight more luchadors including Aerostar, Prince Puma, and the legendary Rey Mysteno Jr. to capture the Lucha Underground Championship.

Sexy Star

Won November 16, 2016

Episode 3.11 | Aztec Warfare III

Matanza remained undefeated since his victory at Aztec Warfare II. In fact, Dario was confident that no one could defeat his brother, that he put Matanza in as #1 in Aztec Warfare III, which led to an elimination by Rey Mysterio. With the monster vanquished, it was Sexy Stars time to shine. Against all odds, Sexy Star landed a perfect diving double foot stomp on Mil Muertes, putting him e through a table to become the first ever female world champion in Lucha Underground.

Johnny Mundo

Won November 23, 2016

Episode 3.12 | Every Woman is Sexy, Every Woman is a Star

Just one week after her historic victory, Sexy Star was contractually bound to defend her title against her bitter dual (and the man who usurped her Gift of the Gods title from her), Johnny Mundo. "The Mayor of Slamtown" resorted to his usual sneaky tactics, and with the help of a crutch-wielding lucha fan and a pair of brass knuckle, Mundo was able to secure the victory and the title. Who was the fan? None other than Worldwide Underground member, Taya.
